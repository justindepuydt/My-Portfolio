Capstone Project

To complete the coursework in the Technical Project Management program, I created a Project Management Plan and attendant documents, calculations and presentations, based on a hypothetical business case for a web development proejct. This 'capstone' project was divided into 3 sections (as below), encompassing, process, communication, and technical skills

The first step in developing this project plan was to created a Project Charter, Requirements Management Plan, RACI Matrix, Requirements Register, and an Agile Product Backlog,

The second step, focusing on communication and stakeholder management, included a Kickoff Meeting (Video), Communications Management Plan, Stakeholder Assessment Matrix, Team Operational Guidelines, and a Leadership Statement,

The final step assumed completion of the Analaysis phase and focused on Earned Value Calculations and a Project Status Update (agenda) and (presentation), Summary Assessment and the Complete Project Management Plan,

ESL Summer Programs

Oxford International Education group

ESl summer programs are in-person educational programs for international teenagers. Students travel in groups from various countries to enjoy time in a US city while learning English in classroom and immersive environments. Programs such as these run on thin margins until they reach scale, so planning and development have to be carefully orchestrated to allow for profit at growth while keeping expenses to a minimum in early iterations. I created the initial programs in San Diego, Los Angeles, and Boston,and updated an existing program in New York. The development phase involves careful budgeting and forecasting, scheduling of limited staff, meetings and regular updates with key stakeholders including creating contingency plans and risk assessments

  • Active Dates: January 2015 - October 2018
